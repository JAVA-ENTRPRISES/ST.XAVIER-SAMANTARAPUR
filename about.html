About Our School

St. Xavier High School in Samantarapur,Bhubaneswar, founded in 2009, is a prominent co-educational English-medium school providing CBSE education up to the senior secondary level (Class XII). Known for a holistic approach, it focuses on academic excellence, all-round personality development, and co-curricular activities like sports and arts.
