Our School Facilities

Providing a world-class environment for the leaders of tomorrow.

🏫

Smart Classrooms

Spacious, well-ventilated rooms equipped with digital smart boards and ergonomic furniture to enhance learning.

🔬

Composite Science Lab

Fully equipped laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology that meet CBSE safety and equipment standards.

💻

Computer Resource Centre

High-speed internet-enabled lab with a 1:1 student-to-computer ratio, focusing on digital literacy and coding.

📚

Library & Reading Room

A vast collection of over 5,000 books, periodicals, and journals with a dedicated quiet zone for research.

⚽

Sports Complex

Facilities for Football, Cricket, and Athletics, along with indoor spaces for Table Tennis and Chess.

🛠️

Composite Skill Lab

Dedicated space for vocational activities like pottery and electronics as per the New Education Policy (NEP).

🚌

GPS Enabled Transport

A fleet of modern buses equipped with GPS tracking and experienced drivers ensuring safe student commutes.

🛡️

Safety & Surveillance

24/7 CCTV monitoring and fire safety equipment installed throughout the campus as per NBC norms.

🩺

Health & Medical Room

A dedicated medical bay with a trained nurse and first-aid facilities for immediate student care.
